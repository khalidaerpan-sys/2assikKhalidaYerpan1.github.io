Midterm Project — Wireframes

Sinners (2025)

This website is my midterm project dedicated to the upcoming movie Sinners (2025). The goal is to present the storyline, cast, gallery, and provide contact info for fans. Target users: movie fans, students, and anyone interested in cinema culture. Purpose: to create a simple and clear site design with consistent structure.
